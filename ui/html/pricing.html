Pricing

Remember all the times you got WA on large hidden testcases and you abandoned your approach to implement the editorial's solution? What if your solution was ALMOST correct?
Understanding why your approach is incorrect is as important as getting an AC.
CF Stress is designed to help you become a better programmer by allowing you to explore your approach till the very end.

Terms and Conditions

  • We do not guarantee that testcases for contests conducted before your purchase would be available. However, if they are, you are free to use them with no extra charge.
  • We do not guarantee that you'd be able to find failing testcase for each and every problem. However, you can rest assured that the generators are written with great care, and if you are on the platinum plan, you can email us whenever you aren't able to find a counter example.
